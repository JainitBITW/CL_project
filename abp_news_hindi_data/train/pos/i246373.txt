A tropical monsoon climate prevails over the drainage basin area of the lake. The lake experiences South–west and North-east monsoons during June to September and November to December respectively with average annual rainfall of 1,238.8 मि॰मी॰, with 72 rainy days. The maximum temperature of 39.9 °से. and minimum temperature of 14 °से. have been recorded. The wind speed varies from 5.3 से 16 मीटर /hour with southerly and southwesterly direction due to the influence of the South–west monsoon and from north and north easterly direction during the rest of the months.
